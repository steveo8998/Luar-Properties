Project Proposal

Luar Properties
Direct Booking Platform

A custom website for listing rental properties and accepting direct bookings — saving on third-party platform fees while giving guests a seamless experience.

Prepared for Nate — Luar Properties March 2026 Peoria, Illinois Prepared by Stephen Odanovich — Aitheos LLC
Overview

The Opportunity

Luar Properties currently lists two rental properties in Peoria, IL through Airbnb. When existing guests want to extend their stay, they're forced to rebook through Airbnb — costing both the guest and Luar Properties unnecessary platform fees.

This project delivers a custom direct-booking website where guests can browse available properties, select dates, and pay securely — all without a middleman. The site will sync in real-time with Airbnb, VRBO, and other platforms via Hostaway, so availability is always up to date across every channel.

The platform is designed to grow. While launching with two properties, the system will make it easy to add new listings as the business expands.

Scope of Work

What We're Building

This MVP delivers everything needed to accept direct bookings from day one. Every feature below is included in the project price.

1

Public-Facing Website

A clean, mobile-first website designed to feel familiar to anyone who has used Airbnb — simple, professional, and easy to navigate.

  • Home page introducing Luar Properties with featured property cards and links to book
  • Individual property pages with full photo galleries, descriptions, amenities, bed/bath counts, square footage, and all details a guest needs to make a decision
  • Contact page with an email form for guest questions and support inquiries
  • Legal and terms page (content provided by Luar Properties)
  • Fully responsive — optimized for phones, tablets, and desktops
2

Booking & Payment System

The core of the platform. Guests select their dates, see a transparent price breakdown, and pay securely — with instant confirmation.

  • Interactive availability calendar showing open and blocked dates in real time
  • Custom day-of-week pricing (different rates for Mon–Sun) calculated automatically
  • Minimum stay enforcement (e.g., 7-night or 30-night minimums per property)
  • Dynamic price breakdown: nightly rates × number of nights + cleaning fee + occupancy tax = total
  • Secure checkout via Stripe — PCI compliant, no card data touches our servers
  • Full payment collected at time of booking — no partial payments or deposits
  • Clear no-refund policy displayed before checkout
3

Hostaway Calendar Integration

Two-way sync between the Luar Properties website and all connected booking platforms — Airbnb, VRBO, and any others managed through Hostaway.

  • Bookings made on Airbnb or VRBO automatically block those dates on the Luar Properties website
  • Bookings made on the Luar Properties website automatically push to Hostaway, blocking those dates on all connected platforms
  • Automated sync runs on a regular schedule to keep availability current
4

Booking Confirmation Email

Guests receive an immediate confirmation email after successful payment with everything they need for their stay.

  • Property photo, name, and full address
  • Check-in and check-out dates with number of nights
  • Line-item receipt: nightly rates, cleaning fee, occupancy tax, and total charged
  • No-refund policy reminder
  • Sent from a branded email address (e.g., bookings@luarproperties.com)
5

Admin Dashboard

A private management panel for you to control every aspect of the business — properties, pricing, and financials — without needing a developer.

  • Secure login (password protected, single admin account)
  • Add, edit, and remove properties with photos, descriptions, bed/bath counts, square footage, and amenities
  • Connect each property to Hostaway via Listing ID for two-way calendar sync, with an optional iCal feed URL for pulling availability from additional sources
  • Drag-and-drop photo upload and management for each property
  • Set custom nightly rates for each day of the week (Monday through Sunday) per property
  • Set cleaning fee and occupancy tax rate per property
  • Set minimum stay requirement per property
  • View all bookings with guest name, email, property, dates, and payment amounts
  • CSV export of booking data with monthly totals broken down by nightly revenue, cleaning fees, and occupancy taxes collected — making tax filing straightforward
6

Testing, Deployment & Handoff

Thorough quality assurance across the entire booking flow, plus a guided walkthrough so you're confident managing the site independently.

  • End-to-end testing of the booking and payment flow with real Stripe test transactions
  • Mobile and tablet responsiveness testing
  • Hostaway sync verification across platforms
  • Production deployment and custom domain setup
  • Guided walkthrough of the admin dashboard with you
  • Written documentation for adding properties, updating pricing, and exporting reports
Technology

How It's Built

The platform is built on modern, industry-standard tools chosen specifically for reliability, low cost, and ease of long-term maintenance.

⚡

Next.js

React framework for the full website and admin dashboard. Fast, SEO-friendly, and used by thousands of production sites.

🗄️

Supabase

Database and image storage. Stores property listings, photos, pricing rules, and booking records securely.

💳

Stripe

Industry-leading payment processor. Handles credit card payments securely with automatic receipt generation.

🔄

Hostaway

Channel manager integration for two-way calendar sync with Airbnb, VRBO, and other booking platforms.

📧

Resend

Transactional email service for sending professional booking confirmations from a branded email address.

🌐

Vercel

Website hosting with automatic SSL, global CDN, and zero-configuration deployments. Built for Next.js.

Boundaries

What's Not Included

The following items are outside the scope of this MVP. Any of these can be quoted separately as future enhancements once the platform is live and performing well.

  • Automated post-booking email sequences (check-in instructions, WiFi details, house rules)
  • Guest user accounts or guest portal
  • Refund or cancellation processing
  • Security deposit collection
  • Cleaning crew scheduling or notifications
  • Multi-language or multi-currency support
  • Blog or content management
  • SEO strategy or marketing campaigns
  • Legal page content (provided by Luar Properties)
  • Hostaway account setup or subscription fees
  • Stripe account setup or transaction fees
  • Multi-admin access — the admin dashboard supports a single owner account and is built exclusively for Luar Properties
  • Multi-tenant or multi-business support — the platform processes all bookings and payments through a single Stripe account connected to one business entity
Design revisions: This proposal includes two rounds of design revisions per page. Additional revision rounds can be accommodated at an agreed-upon hourly rate.
